General Tournament Rules

Tournament process:

  • Team Captains join the tournament through a Google Form which is supplied by D-LAN. Your team has to be complete throughout the tournament, meaning you can have no vacant positions when entering the tournament.
    • If your team wants to have a substitute player, in case of a player not showing up or having to leave the LAN early, the Team Captain can register the player to the referee at the actual LAN itself. This has to happen at least 30 minutes before the actual tournament starts.
    • Each player can be a part of one team only. This includes substitute players as well.
    • The teams will communicate in their assigned discord-channel during the whole tournament.
  • All players must have a legitimate D-LAN Computer Ticket close by for tournaments during D-LAN. This does not apply during D-WAN.
    • This means that if you are a +1 to someone's LiU-ID or someone else is paying your ticket, they have to be close by to verify your credibility.
  • All remaining players must be close by throughout the whole tournament.
    • This does not apply to the teams that has been eliminated or disqualified.
    • IMPORTANT: If the other team has not accepted or joined your game within 10 minutes, you are to report this to the referee. If the other team is absent they will be notified by some means. If the other team fails to respond within 5 minutes, they will be disqualified.
  • Team Captains have the responsibility to make contact with each other and invite their teams to the game.
  • The winning team has to send a screenshot of the results to the referee in the respective tournament #results discord channel and the bracket will shortly be updated.
  • Team captains must update the referee beforehand and provide full details on players and stand-ins. This includes full name and player-ID.
  • Usage of unsportsmanlike, disrespectful, sexist or derogatory terms during the tournament is strictly forbidden. This includes In-Game nicknames, In-Game item name tags or In-Game chat.
  • If the game would freeze or crash mid-match, the referee will decide whether the match will be restarted or have any other outcome.
  • Disconnect mid-draft will result in a restarted draft face with the same picks and bans as up to that point.
  • If a player were to be disconnected mid-match:
    • The game will pause and the player who disconnected will have 5 minutes to reconnect. After 5 minutes the game will start again and the team with the disconnected member will play with only four members.
      • Players are allowed to pause the game if they have a legitimate reason, such as disconnects.
      • The disconnected player are allowed to try and reconnect even when the game has started again.
    • Disconnecting on purpose or pausing the game without a legitimate reason, will be considered cheating and result in disqualification.
  • Send all questions to turnering@d-lan.se before and after the LAN. During the LAN, send the questions via Discord in the channel #tournament-questions, or ping / DM @Turnering & Biljett through the Discord server.

Bracket rules:

  • Tournament format is Single Elimination.
  • Team size: 5 players, of which at least 3 are LiU-students
  • The first matches will be randomized to determine which teams will face each other.
  • All matches will be played in a best of 1, except the finals (counting the bronze match) which will be played in a best of 3.
    • This means that the losing teams of the semi-finals will be facing each other in the bronze match to determine the 3rd place team (this will not be seen in the bracket)
    • 3rd place -- Bronze finals winner
    • 2nd place -- Finals loser
    • 1st place -- Finals winner
  • Coin toss will decide which team will start on each side
  • The winner of the coin toss will decide which side they want
  • In the best of 3 matches, the teams will switch sides in the second and third round

Game specific rules:

  • Maps: Anubis, Nuke, Ancient, Mirage, Inferno, Overpass, Vertigo
  • Note: The map pool will be the Active Duty map pool and therefore might be updated before the tournament.
  • Team size: 5 players
  • Player agents are not allowed
  • Best-of-One (Bo1) Matches
  • Team A starts the process and the order of the ban / pick is as follows:
    • Team A removes one map
    • Team B removes one map
    • Team A removes one map
    • Team B removes one map
    • Team A removes one map
    • Team B removes one map
    • Remaining map is being played
    • Winner of knife round gets to pick sides
  • Best-of-Three (Bo3) Matches:
    • Team A removes one map
    • Team B removes one map
    • Team A removes one map
    • Team B removes one map
    • Team A picks one map
    • Team B picks one map
    • Remaining map is being played as a decider, if required
    • Every map starts with a knife round, winner of knife round gets to pick sides
  • In case of a draw after all 30 rounds have been played, an overtime will be played best of 6 with $10,000 start money. Teams will continue to play overtime until a winner has been found.

Illegal scripting:

  • Generally, scripts that are made ingame are allowed, some examples include buy, toggle and demo scripts.
    • No recoil scripts
    • Rate changers (Lag scripts)
    • FPS scripts
    • Bunny hop scripts

Sponsored by

Bracket rules:

  • Tournament format is Double Elimination.
  • Single player tournament: Any D-LAN attendee may enter the tournament
  • The first matches will be randomized to determine which players will face each other
  • Coin toss will decide who starts on which side. The player in the upper bracket slot chooses either heads or tails and the referee performs the flip. The winner of the coin toss chooses if they want to start white or black.
    • Each game alternates who is white or black, so if you played white the first game you play black in the second and then white in the third.
  • All games are 5+3 time format
  • All games are played in Lichess
  • Player from the winning bracket will get one point advantage in the finals
  • All matches will be played in Best of 3, except the finals (counting the bronze match) which will be played in Best of 5
    • This means that the losing players of the semi-finals will be facing each other in the bronze match to determine the 3rd place (this will not be seen in the bracket)
    • 3rd place -- Bronze finals winner
    • 2nd place -- Finals loser
    • 1st place -- Finals winner

General rules:

  • You have the responsibility to invite the right opponent and play the match
    • Both players have the responsibility to follow all the rules stated in this document, contact the referee in case of conflict
